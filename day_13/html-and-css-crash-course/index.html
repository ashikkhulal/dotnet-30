It's a me, Mario!

Hey there, it's me, Mario! I'm the famous video game character created by the amazing Shigeru Miyamoto. Oh, it's-a so exciting to be here and share my adventures with you! I first appeared in 1985 in the groundbreaking game "Super Mario Bros.," and since then, I've been on countless thrilling quests to rescue my beloved Princess Peach from the clutches of that dastardly Bowser. I'm always on the move, jumping and running across platforms, collecting power-ups, and facing off against tricky enemies. You can't miss my signature red cap, blue overalls, and mustache – they make me stand out in every crowd! Throughout the years, I've had the honor of entertaining generations of gamers, and it fills my heart with joy to see how much you all love me and my adventures. From classic platformers to sports and racing games, I've done it all! So let's-a go and have some fun together, because it's-a me, Mario! Woohoo!

Join Today!

The Mario Club is a vibrant and enthusiastic community that brings together fans of the iconic video game character, Mario, and his adventures in the Mushroom Kingdom. With a rich history spanning decades, the club provides a nostalgic and inclusive space for gamers of all ages to bond over their shared love for the beloved plumber. From classic side-scrolling platformers to modern 3D adventures, members celebrate the enduring legacy of Mario, Luigi, Princess Peach, and their colorful cast of friends and foes. Regular meetups and events offer opportunities for friendly competitions, game discussions, and cosplay gatherings, fostering a sense of camaraderie and excitement among enthusiasts. Whether you're a seasoned gamer or a newcomer to the world of Mario, the Mario Club warmly welcomes everyone to join the journey and create cherished memories in this fantastical gaming universe.
